About me

I'm a young man who hailed from the magical land of Los Angeles, I moved up to Vancouver for a while (because of my dad), and now I'm looking to get back into Los Angeles to pursue my passions of film-making, video game production, coding, and not being unemployed! I'm an eager, young man looking to make a difference in this world and I'm still figuring out exactly how I'll do that. I have so many skills and interests, I don't know what to do with myself! The least of which are: Coding, Guitar, Drums, Video Games, Film-Making, Improv Comedy, Close-Up Magic, American Sign Language, and oh so many more. I'm a jack of many-a-trade with various degrees of proficiency. But I'm quick to learn. I love learning and picking things apart to find out how they work!
